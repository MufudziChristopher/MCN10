Overview

Risk Mitigation Consultants (RMC) was born from a desire to create more than ‘just another risk company’. The four founding members have a shared vision based on the virtues of; respect, integrity, courage, honesty, loyalty and self-control. This is the foundation of RMC.
The core purpose of RMC is to analyse and mitigate risk. To protect you, your company and your community while also protecting (the lives and livelihoods) of our own. This doesn’t mean more cost to you, it means better cost control by us with a commitment to paying our thoroughly vetted and approved consultants a greater share than the industry ‘norm’ that it’s known for.
We invest in our people on the payroll so that they can invest in your safety with the peace of mind that comes with knowing their lives and livelihoods are protected too.
Our highly skilled leadership team has a combined 50+ years of safety and security experience.All are PSIRA graded, highly experienced in analysing and mitigating security risks, crime prevention, tactical response and fully trained in close protection techniques.
RMC is Level 4 BEE compliant.
